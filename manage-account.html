Delete Your Account

Last updated: August 5, 2025

At SafelyRecycle (a product of SafelyCFL), we respect your privacy and are committed to giving you full control over your data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated information, please review the steps below.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

  • Your personal data will be permanently erased from our servers.
  • This includes your profile, preferences, usage data, and any submitted content.
  • This action is irreversible. Your account cannot be recovered.

How to Delete Your Account

To request account deletion, please send an email to:

Email: team@safelycfl.com

Subject Line: Delete My Account

Please include the following in your message:

  • Your full name (optional)
  • The email address linked to your account
  • Your sign-up method (Google, Apple, Email, etc.)

We will confirm and process your request within 7 business days.

Don’t Have an Account?

If you haven't registered or logged in, no action is needed. We do not store personally identifiable information for guest users.

Your Data Rights

In accordance with regulations such as GDPR and CCPA, you may:

  • Access your stored data
  • Update or correct your data
  • Request account deletion
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Can I Reactivate My Account?

No. Once your data is deleted, your account cannot be restored. You may create a new account at any time.
